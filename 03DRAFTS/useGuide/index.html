User Guide

Feel free to spend as much time as you want in each exhibit. The music in each exhibit will keep playing until the song is done, but if you want to move on just hit spacebar to pause the music and navigate to the next exhibit using the menu in the top left.

Exhibit 01 - Circles in Physics Engine

  • - Nothing will happen if you do not click
  • - SPACEBAR: Pause/Play music

Exhibit 02 - Eye Tracking with ML

  • - Enable camera access for exhibit to work
  • - Adjust webcam so it sees your face
  • - SPACEBAR: Pause/Play music

Exhibit 03 - 3D Scene with WebGL

  • - “r” key: resets position of the camera
  • - “t” key: toggle the camera’s rotation
  • - Scroll to zoom in and out of scene
  • - SPACEBAR: Pause/Play music
